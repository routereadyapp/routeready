Import from ShowingTime
Copy and paste the text from one or more ShowingTime confirmation emails. RouteReady extracts the address, date and time automatically — and ignores declined showings.
How to use
1
Open your ShowingTime confirmation email and select all the text (press & hold → Select All).
2
Copy the text and paste it below. You can paste multiple emails at once.
3
Tap Extract Showings. Confirmed showings are loaded automatically. Declined ones are ignored.
4
Tap Send to Plan My Route to optimize the order with Google Maps.
